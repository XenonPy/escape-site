are you up for the challenge?

ESCAPE is an innovative game to test the limits of your skill, speed, and patience. Its minimal design leaves one main objective: ESCAPE by conquering all the levels. Enjoy the rewards of working to pass hard levels, and completing achievements along the way. Still not hard enough? Just turn INSANE MODE on in settings and the rest is history.

No PC? No problem! ESCAPE supports a variety of platforms, and you can play on mobile with built-in touchscreen controls. ESCAPE also has very regular updates: bugfixes/minor changes are generally released every week or so, if not, a big feature is probably in the works! You can support the creation of new levels/features by donating on itch.io (coming soon).
